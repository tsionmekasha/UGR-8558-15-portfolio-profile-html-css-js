TechPulse Branding Project

The TechPulse Branding Project involved designing a modern and professional logo for a fictional tech company, "TechPulse." The project included creating mockups of the logo on various platforms and merchandise, such as T-shirts, mugs, laptops, and mobile app icons. The goal was to showcase how the branding would appear in real-world applications while maintaining consistency and an innovative feel.

Technologies Used:

  • Adobe Illustrator
  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Creative Cloud Assets
